How to gain weight ? (CICO)

You can only gain weight (fat/muscle) when you are in a caloric surplus. Eat more and gain weight. Also sleep for minimum 8 hours. Drink water too.

But sir, I eat a lot and I still dont gain jackshit !

You are overestimating the amount you are eating. Psychology has a big influence on eating and hence makes it hard for many people to eat more even if they are eating less unless they are used to it.

I dont wanna get fat! I wanna be big but I wanna be jacked !

Be in a weightlifting routine which has a high volume split.

I wanna get strong! I dont care about being fat.

Follow a powerlifting routine with high frequency and eat a lot. Looks arent a good indicator of strength since muscles are usually hidden by fat.

I am working out a lot but cant seem to get big or see any increase in my strength!

Eat more and sleep more.

I dont have access to gym/weights.

Follow a bodyweight routine. You will progress slower since progression isnt linear and stalls early.
